What is Tokenization?

The process of splitting raw text into relevant chunks.

What does the Counter class do?

Counts the instances of elements in a given list.

What are stop-words?

Words that have a high occurrence but don't provide much, if any, meaning on their own.

What is text normalization?

Replacing tokens with a canonical, or standard form that is more consistent and connects those that might not be easily obvious as having the same purpose. Ex: Color, color, COLOR, colour. Technically, all of those are unique tokens, but are all practically the same.

What are n-grams?

N-length sequence of adjacent terms.
